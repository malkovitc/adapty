## Logo Design Guidelines

All logos in the marquee follow these design principles:
- **Color**: Grayscale (#64748B or similar muted gray)
- **Format**: SVG preferred, WebP/PNG acceptable
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, professional
- **Size**: Optimized for ~120-160px width, ~40-80px height
- **Effect**: Starts at 60% opacity with grayscale, animates to full color on hover
